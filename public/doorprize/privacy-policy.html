Privacy policy

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

Automatically collected data: IP address and browser characteristics are collected automatically when you visit this (or any other) web site. Like many other web sites, WheelOfNames.com uses Google Analytics. This product can identify you as a first-time or repeat visitor, the length of your visit, and similar. Read more about Google Analytics data collection.
Data you may choose to supply: If you add names to the wheel by typing into the text-box, we will receive those names. If you import names from one of your spreadsheets, we will also receive those names, and the list of file names in your Google Drive account. If you choose to save a wheel by clicking the Save button, we store the names and settings for that wheel in Google Cloud Platform so you can retrieve them later by clicking the Open button. If you submit the feedback form, we receive all the data you have entered into that form.
How your data is used: First, your data is used to fulfill your request. This means that we draw the names you entered on the wheel, or display the list of files from your Google Drive so that you can pick one of them. Second, we also use the data to improve the service by measuring user behavior on the web site. Most of these measurements are done in aggregate across many users, as allowed by Google Analytics. We also use the names you have entered on the wheel to understand what you are using the web site for and improve it for that use case. Third, if you submit an email address in the feedback form, we may use that email address to get back to you if we have follow-up questions on your feedback.
How your data is safeguarded: Your data is stored in Google Analytics, Google Spreadsheets and Google Cloud Platform, under an account accessible only by Martin Omander. For details on how data is managed in those products by Google, see Google's privacy policy. Your data is not shared, rented or sold to any other third parties.

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time. You can do the following at any time by contacting us via support@omanderconsulting.com:
  • See what data we have about you, if any.
  • Change/correct any data we have about you.
  • Have us delete any data we have about you, within the limits of what is technically possible.
  • Express any concern you have about our use of your data.

Security

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline.
Wherever we collect sensitive information (such as email address), that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. You can verify this by looking for a lock icon in the address bar of your browser when accessing WheelOfNames.com.
While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only the maintainer of this website (Martin Omander) has access to personally identifiable information. The computers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure Google data center.
If you feel that we are not abiding by this privacy policy, you should contact us immediately at support@omanderconsulting.com.
